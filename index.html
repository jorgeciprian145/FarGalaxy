Privacy Policy — Far Galaxy

Last updated: March 2026

Far Galaxy is a mobile application designed to help users stay focused through a space-themed productivity experience.

This Privacy Policy explains how information may be collected, used, and protected when you use the app.

Information Collection

Far Galaxy does not require users to create an account and does not collect personally identifiable information such as name, email address, or phone number.

However, certain non-personal information may be collected automatically to improve the app experience.

This may include:

  • Device type
  • Operating system version
  • App performance data
  • Anonymous usage statistics

Local Data Storage

Some app data such as progress, credits, or unlocked items may be stored locally on the user's device in order to provide the core functionality of the app.

In-App Purchases

Far Galaxy may offer optional in-app purchases such as virtual credits or ships.

All payments are processed securely through the Google Play Store using Google Play's billing system. Far Galaxy does not collect or store payment information.

Advertising

The app may display advertisements provided by third-party advertising services such as Google AdMob.

These services may use device identifiers, including the Android Advertising ID, to provide and measure ads.

Users can reset or disable the use of the Advertising ID in their device settings.

Third-Party Services

The app may rely on services provided by third parties including:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google Play Billing
  • Google AdMob

These services may collect information in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Data Processing

Some information may be processed by third-party services on servers located outside the user's country.

These services operate under their own privacy policies and data protection practices.

Children’s Privacy

Far Galaxy is not directed specifically toward children under the age of 13.

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be reflected on this page.
